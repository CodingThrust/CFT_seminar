This file record some fun papers on CFT, worth of reading.

# Quantum many-body physics orientation:

1. [Entanglement entropy and conformal field theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/0905.4013): Critical law of entanglement enyropy. Put forward the cardy-calabrese formula: $S_{\mathrm{vN}} = \frac{c}{3}\ln(\frac{L}{\pi}\sin(\frac{\pi l}{L}))$, relating entanglement entropy to twist operator's scaling dimensions.

2. [Universal noninteger ‘‘ground-state degeneracy’’ in critical quantum systems](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.67.161): Give the $g$ boundary entanglement entropy in BCFT (Boundary CFT).
   
3. [Conformal invariance, the central charge, and universal finite-size amplitudes at criticality](https://journals.aps.org/prl/pdf/10.1103/PhysRevLett.56.742) and [Universal term in the free energy at a critical point and the conformal anomaly](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.56.746): Both derive the ground state energy have $\frac{-\pi c}{6L}$ offset, originating from the Casimir effect.

4. [Universal Entropy of Conformal Critical Theories on a Klein Bottle](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.119.261603): A klein bottle spacetime manifold rather than usual torus manifold will have an additional term $g$, which is related to non-negative integers (NIM-rep) and quantum dimension of primary fields.

# Tensor network orientation:
1. [Precision reconstruction of rational CFT from exact fixed point tensor network](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2311.18005): Approximate fixed-point (FP) tensors are widely used to extract the conformal data, and is used to construct 2D rational CFT in this paper.
2. [Extraction of conformal data in critical quantum spin chains using the Koo-Saleur formula](https://journals.aps.org/prb/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevB.96.245105): Using lattice model's data to obtain the property of field theory.
3. [Tensor networks, quantum spin chains, and quantum field theory](https://uwspace.uwaterloo.ca/items/584177f2-31fa-4354-9ea2-118ad8fc5708) PhD thesis by Yijian Zou.
